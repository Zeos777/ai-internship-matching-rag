# Sample JD: AI Application Intern

## Responsibilities

- Track and evaluate AI tools for business scenarios.
- Analyze operational pain points and identify AI application opportunities.
- Build small-scale AI workflow prototypes.
- Prepare documentation, reports, and internal training materials.

## Requirements

- Interest in AI tools and applied AI scenarios.
- Basic understanding of prompt engineering or LLM applications.
- Familiarity with Python or data analysis workflows.
- Ability to write clear reports and communicate with non-technical stakeholders.
- Experience with machine learning, RAG, or automation tools is a plus.
