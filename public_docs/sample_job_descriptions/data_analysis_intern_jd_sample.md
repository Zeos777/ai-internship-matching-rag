# Sample JD: Data Analysis Intern

## Responsibilities

- Collect, clean, and analyze business data.
- Build dashboards or analytical reports.
- Support operational decision-making through quantitative analysis.
- Communicate findings clearly to business teams.

## Requirements

- Familiarity with Excel and Python.
- Basic understanding of statistics, regression, and visualization.
- Experience with data cleaning and model evaluation is a plus.
- Strong documentation and presentation skills.
